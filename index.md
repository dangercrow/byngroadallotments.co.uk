## Welcome to Byng Road Allotments

Byng Road Allotments was one of the first allotments established in 1939 as a war-time allotment by the British Ministry of Agriculture and Fisheries. We started life in the adjacent field, where Noah’s Ark Children’s Hospice and the Environment Centre is currently located. We moved to our current location in 1952.

We occupy just over 3.5 acres and have over 90 plots. In 2023 we introduced three starter plots for people on our waiting list to experience allotment gardening while waiting for a plot to become available.

Our facilities include water supply, a communal plot with an apple orchard, a small wildlife plot, on-site car parks and a chemical toilet during the spring and summer months.
