# Annual General Meeting (AGM)

Our Constitution requires that and Annual General Meeting (AGM) must be held once a year. All BRAG members are encouraged and welcome to attend.

At an AGM, the committee will discuss its performance, present the financial accounts, vote on any issues that require changes to the handbook and/or Constitution. Tenants will also have the opportunity to discuss any general issues with the committee.

Any BRAG member may nominate themselves for the BRAG Committee by completing and submitting the BRAG Committee Nomination Form before an AGM. Please refer to the BRAG Constitution and BRAG Rules for Committee Elections for further information.
